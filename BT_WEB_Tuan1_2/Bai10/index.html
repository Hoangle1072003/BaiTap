IUH

The predecessor of the Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh City was the Go Vap Vocational School founded by the Don Bosco monks on 11/11/1956 in Hanh Thong Commune, Go Vap District, Gia Dinh Province. In 1968, the School was renamed Don Bosco Technical Middle School Private School.
